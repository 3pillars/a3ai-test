# Trading Checklist - Based on Article Rules

## Pre-Trade Checklist (Before Entering Any Trade)

- [ ] **Strategy Confirmed** — Have I defined my entry trigger?
- [ ] **Area of Interest Identified** — Where on the chart am I looking?
- [ ] **Risk Calculated** — Is this ≤1% of total account?
- [ ] **Stop Loss (SL) Set** — Exact price level documented
- [ ] **Take Profit (TP) Set** — Exact price level documented
- [ ] **One-Sentence Reason** — Can I explain why I'm taking this trade in one sentence?
- [ ] **Not Emotional** — Am I FOMO'ing or chasing? (If yes, DON'T TRADE)
- [ ] **Liquidity Check** — Is this a Top 10 coin? (Avoid shitcoins)
- [ ] **Time Available** — Do I have time to manage this (or will I be in meetings?) → Use Limit Orders if busy

## Automation Setup (For Busy Schedule)

- [ ] Use Limit Orders (set and forget)
- [ ] Use OCO (One-Cancels-the-Other) orders
- [ ] Set price alerts instead of watching charts
- [ ] Only check positions 2-3x per day (pre-market, lunch, end of day)

✓ Keep your job — salary = psychological safety net
✓ 1% risk max — seatbelt keeps you alive
