# Balaji Srinivasan - The Internet Age

Source: Video takeaways summary

## Key Thesis
- Western civilization (US, UK, Western Europe) in decline due to unsustainable debt
- Internet emerging as new global power
- "Internet-first" rebuilding will take years to decades

## Core Concepts

### Taking the L
- Acknowledge current system is a lost cause
- "Write it off to zero"
- Focus on rebuilding

### Survival Guide: Liquidate, Emigrate, Accelerate
1. **Liquidate** - Sell assets
2. **Emigrate** - Move to more favorable locations  
3. **Accelerate** - Do it now before restrictions

### AI's Impact
- AI doesn't take jobs - it takes the job of the previous AI
- Turns individuals into "CEOs" with powerful tools
- New job categories: "proctoring and verification" (due to deepfakes)
- Acceleration cycle is speeding up

### China vs The Internet
- Conflict: centralized AI (China) vs decentralized encrypted internet
- Clash: total state vs sovereign individual

### Internet Speed
- Google built in 20 years
- OpenAI built in 10 years
- New societies can be built at internet speed

## Timeline
- Shift to "internet-first" will take years if not decades
- Debt crisis approaching: US/UK sovereign default
- Capital controls ("Berlin Wall of money") to trap wealth

## Implications for Individuals
- Diversify assets (not just USD)
- Stay liquid
- Embrace new technology early
- Consider geographic flexibility
